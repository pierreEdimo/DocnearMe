DocNearMe

Our Mission: to help everyone,to gather Informations about Doctors and hospitals around them and get the care they need

The Story

I had the idea of DocNearMe from
a friend who wanted me to build
a management system for hospitals
I told myself it would be useless
instead i decided to build this
Platform where i gather every
hospitals in every cities so
people can look for a hospital,
Or a doctor where they live

The Mission

In small countries especially
thirld world countries ,
it is very difficult to look
for a doctor on google, most of
them i believe , doesn't have
an online presence. I remember
as a kid i did not often go to
a doctor. It is something that
need to change. I want to gather
all of them on this website
and allow people to look
for them , to find information
about them, and to contact them
